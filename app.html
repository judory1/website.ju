Hilingual

I’ve been using this app quite often these days, and fun fact—it was actually created by a friend of mine. It gives you a daily topic and lets you write short entries in English, which you can then revise with the help of AI.

It might sound a bit simple at first, but what makes it special is the social aspect. You can share your writing with friends and read each other’s posts, which makes the whole experience much more engaging. I especially like knowing that my friends are reading what I write—it keeps me motivated to keep going.
