Dubrovnik, Croatia

Dubrovnik is a place that stays in my memory long after the trip ends. I visited in 2024 with friends I met during my exchange in the UK, and it was our first trip together, which made the experience even more meaningful. The Mediterranean food was excellent, and the people were very warm and friendly. The sea was incredibly clear, and the old town was beautiful and full of history.

One of the most unforgettable moments was watching the night view from the mountain. I recommend Dubrovnik because it is warm, peaceful, and truly beautiful.
