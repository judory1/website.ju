1. PROJECT HAIL MARY

Project Hail Mary is an SF film based on the novel of the same name by Andy Weir, the bestselling author best known for The Martian. It was released in Korea on March 18, so it’s still very fresh, and honestly, it was such a treat to watch a sci-fi film that feels almost like a modern-day fairy tale. The story keeps you engaged from beginning to end without ever feeling exhausting — instead, it constantly makes you look forward to the next scene.
Ryan Gosling’s performance is, unsurprisingly, excellent, and the humor sprinkled throughout the film had the entire theater laughing together at several moments. If you’re in the mood for a heartwarming yet genuinely entertaining film, Project Hail Mary is absolutely worth your time.

2. 헤어질 결심 (Decision to Leave)

Decision to Leave stands out for its quiet yet intense atmosphere. It tells a complex story in a very subtle and elegant way. The emotions feel layered, and nothing is overly explained, which makes it even more engaging. It’s a film that lingers with you in a very understated way.
