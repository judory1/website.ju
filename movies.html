1. Interstellar

Interstellar is one of those movies that stays with you long after it ends. It blends science and emotion in a way that feels both epic and personal. The visuals are stunning, but what really stands out is the story about love and time. It’s the kind of film that makes you sit back and think.

At the same time, it makes space feel surprisingly familiar while inviting you to reflect on what “love” really means. I hope it gives you a moment to think about that as well. Even with a runtime of over three hours, I’ve found myself coming back to it more than four times.

2. 헤어질 결심 (Decision to Leave)

Decision to Leave stands out for its quiet yet intense atmosphere. It tells a complex story in a very subtle and elegant way. The emotions feel layered, and nothing is overly explained, which makes it even more engaging. It’s a film that lingers with you in a very understated way.
