1. Yukon

Yukon stands out for its smooth, low-end groove that carries the entire track. The bassline is especially satisfying, and it creates a calm but immersive mood. I’ve been a long-time fan of Justin Bieber, so there’s also a sense of familiarity and comfort when I listen to it. It’s one of those songs that feels simple at first, but keeps pulling you back in.

2. 404 (New Era)

404 (New Era) is a perfect mix of house music and K-pop, which makes it feel both fresh and exciting. I’ve always enjoyed house music, so hearing it blended with a K-pop sound is especially interesting. The beat is energetic and uplifting, which instantly puts me in a better mood. It’s the kind of track that naturally gives you a boost.
