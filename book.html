Dopamine Nation

Dopamine Nation is a surprisingly eye-opening read, especially in a world full of endless scrolling. At some point, I realized I was spending way too much time on reels and getting used to constant dopamine hits, which didn’t feel right. That’s what led me to pick up this book, and it turned out to be more helpful than I expected. It offers a clear and honest perspective on balance, habits, and how to take back control of your attention.
